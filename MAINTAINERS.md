## Review Service Levels

- Initial PR triage target: within 5 business days
- Follow-up review target: within 5 business days after updates

## Escalation Paths

- Contribution disputes: open an issue and request maintainer input
- Code of Conduct incidents: follow [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Security concerns: follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)

## Becoming A Maintainer

Potential maintainers should demonstrate:

- Consistent, high-quality contributions
- Constructive review participation
- Alignment with community values and governance

Appointment is made by existing maintainers/org admins.
